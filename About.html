About Us

CodeClan Nigeria is a tech community filled with amazing and passionate software developers and tech enthusiasts. At CodeClan Nigeria.

We help polish newbie developers into competent and qualified software developers by Connecting them to mentors and volunteer developers who bring out their time to follow up these mentees allocating different tasks to them, and helping them debug and solve coding errors together.

Codeclan Nigeria has so many amazing developers and designers that are willing to coach you till you become competent enough to apply for jobs and contribute to the Industry. We would love to hear from newbie developers that would love to join the community. We have the finest tech gurus working in various tech industry across the country.
